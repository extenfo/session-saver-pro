Session Saver Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Session Saver Pro is a tab & window session manager for Chrome. It lets you save your current windows and tabs as a session, search sessions by URL, title, or domain, and restore sessions into new windows.

Summary: All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data we collect

  • We do not collect personal data on any server.
  • All session data (tab URLs, titles, window states) and settings are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

Data we share

  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.
  • No external analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks are used.

Data retention

  • Session data remains on your device until you manually delete it.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.
  • You can delete any saved session at any time from within the extension.

Permissions justification

  • storage — store saved sessions and user settings locally on your device.
  • tabs — read open tabs (URL and title) to save and restore sessions.
  • windows — manage browser windows to restore saved sessions into new windows.
  • alarms — schedule autosave at user-defined intervals to prevent data loss.
  • <all_urls> — required to save and restore tabs for any website you have open.

Remote code

  • This extension does not download or execute remote code.
  • All executable code is bundled within the extension package.

Changes to this policy

  • We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
